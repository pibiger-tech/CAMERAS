# FPV-001_M FPV Camera

The **FPV-001_M** is a compact and lightweight FPV camera designed for racing drones, delivering high-definition and low-latency analog video output.

## Key Features

- Ultra-low illumination: F1.0 / 0.000001 LUX — excellent night vision performance
- Ultra-low latency: ≤ 30 ms
- Wide input voltage range: 5 V – 40 V
- Super WDR (HDR): clear visibility in complex or backlit conditions
- OSD menu support for on-camera parameter adjustment

## Interface & Wiring

### Pin Definition (5-pin Connector)

| Pin | Signal | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | POWER | DC 5 V – 40 V |
| 2 | GND | Ground |
| 3 | CVBS | Analog video signal output |
| 4 | GND | Ground |
| 5 | OSD | OSD control |

## OSD Menu

Press the middle button on the menu board briefly to enter OSD settings. Remove the OSD menu board after completing the settings.
